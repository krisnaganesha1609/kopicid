Here is Our Menu Description :

Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffee is a drink made by mixing equal parts instant coffee, sugar, and hot water. The mixture is then whipped into a cream and then added to cold or hot milk.

Fresh Cappuccinno

Cappuccinno is a typical Italian drink made from espresso and milk, but there are other references that mention that kapucino originated from the coffee beans of the Turkish army left behind after the war led by Kara Mustapha Pasha in Vienna, Austria against the joint Polish-Germanian army.

Tasty Milkshake

Milk shake is a cold drink made from a mixture of milk, ice cream and flavored syrup whipped until foamy.In addition to being shaken in a blender, milk shakes can be made using a shaker with a lid.

Healthy Juice

Juice is a drink made from the extraction or pressing of natural fluids contained in fruits and vegetables. Here we serve any kind fruit juice.

Spirit Wine

Wine is an alcoholic drink made from fermenting grapes or other fruits. Due to a natural chemical balance, grapes can ferment without added sugar, acids, enzymes, water or other nutrients.

Delicious Sandwich

Sandwich is a food which usually consists of vegetables, cheese or meat that is sliced, placed on or between slices of bread, or rather any food in which two or more slices of bread serve as a container or wrapper for a type of food.

French Fries

French fries are a dish made from pieces of potato that are fried in hot cooking oil.

Big Steak

A steak is a large piece of meat, usually beef. Red meat, chicken breast and fish are often cut into steaks. Most steaks are cut perpendicular to the muscle fibers, adding to the texture of the meat.
